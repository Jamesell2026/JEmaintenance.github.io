What We Do

Professional Services

Experienced across all major trades — from emergency repairs to full renovations and outdoor projects. One call, every job covered.

Plumbing

Burst pipes, leaks, full bathroom installations, boiler servicing, tap replacements, shower fitting and all domestic plumbing repairs. Fast response for emergencies — we'll get you sorted same day where possible.

Electrical

New sockets and switches, consumer unit upgrades, lighting installations, outdoor electrics, fault finding and rewiring. All electrical work carried out safely, professionally and to current regulations.

Bricklaying

Garden walls, driveway edging, extensions, repointing, steps and structural brickwork repairs. Solid foundations, clean coursework and a finish built to last — whether it's a small garden wall or a full extension.

Carpentry

Door hanging and fitting, skirting boards, architrave, fitted wardrobes, staircases, fencing, gate construction and bespoke woodwork. Precision cuts and clean finishes on every job, no matter the scale.

Tiling

Bathrooms, kitchens, utility rooms, conservatories and outdoor areas. Wall tiles, floor tiles, mosaic and large-format slabs. Precise cutting, professional grouting and waterproofing done properly from the start.

Painting & Decorating

Interior and exterior painting, feature walls, wallpapering and full room makeovers. Thorough surface preparation, clean edges and a professional finish that actually lasts. Domestic and commercial properties covered.

Decking & Patios

Composite and timber decking, block paving, porcelain and natural stone patios. Full ground preparation, accurate levelling and professional laying. Transform your outdoor space into something you actually want to use.

Landscaping & Garden Work

Garden clearances, lawn laying, raised beds, sleeper borders, gravel and bark installations, fencing and pergolas. Whether you need a full garden redesign or a tidy-up, we plan and deliver it properly.

General Repairs & Maintenance

Odd jobs, handyman work, ongoing property maintenance contracts and anything in between. If something needs fixing, replacing or improving — give us a call. No job is too small and we'll always give you an honest price.
